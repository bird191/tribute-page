Dave Ramsey

1960-Present

From bankruptcy to financially successful

David Lawrence Ramsey III (September 3, 1960 to Present) is an American radio personality who offers financial advice.
He is CEO of Ramsey Solutions and has helped countless people manage money better.


"You will live like no one else today, later you can live like no one else."

-Dave Ramsey

TIMELINE

  • 3 September 1960- David Lawrence Ramsey III was born in Antioch, Tennessee
  • 1978- Dave Ramsey started selling real estate.
  • 1986- Amasses a portfolio of over $4 million
  • 1988- filed for bankruptcy
  • experienced several years of financial recovery and after gave financial advice to others
  • 1992- Wrote the book Financial Peace
  • 1992-Starts The Dave Ramsey Show, a talk show for financial advice
  • 1994- Taught Financial Peace University
  • 2014- Ramsey Solutions was started
  • 2015- Inducted into the National Radio Hall of Fame
